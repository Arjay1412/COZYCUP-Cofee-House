About Us:

CozyCup Coffee House began as a small, neighborhood café in 2010 with a simple goal: to create a warm and inviting space where the community could enjoy high-quality coffee. We're passionate about sourcing the best beans, crafting delicious blends, and providing a cozy atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

Meet the Team:

Rick Sanchez, our dedicated owner, whose love for coffee and community drives everything we do.

Morty Smith, our skilled barista, who brings a personal touch to every cup he serves.

Summer Smith, our friendly pastry chef, who creates the delicious baked goods that pair perfectly with our coffee.

Café's Mission:

Our mission is to create a welcoming environment where coffee enthusiasts can relax, connect, and enjoy the finest brews. We are committed to quality in every cup and to making CozyCup a place where everyone feels at home
